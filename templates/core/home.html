How It Works

1. Report

Victims can report stolen phones quickly without creating an account. Just enter IMEI, phone details, and proof of ownership.

2. Verify

Buyers can instantly check a phone’s IMEI before purchase to confirm it’s not reported stolen.

3. Protect

Transparency protects buyers, supports law enforcement, and helps reduce stolen phone sales in the community.

Why IMEI Shield Matters

By making stolen phones harder to resell, we reduce theft, protect buyers, and empower communities to stay safe. Together, we can make second-hand markets more trustworthy.
