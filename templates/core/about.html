About NoStolen

Check Before You Buy. Report When You Lose.

Why No Accounts?

We believe in fast, frictionless use. Victims can report stolen devices and buyers can check phones instantly, without the barrier of creating an account.

This approach encourages more people to use the system while still ensuring integrity through IMEI validation and proof of ownership checks.

IMEI Validation

Every phone has a unique 15-digit IMEI number. NoStolen automatically validates the IMEI format using the Luhn algorithm. Users are warned immediately if the input is incorrect, preventing errors.

Impact

  • Reduces the risk for second-hand phone buyers.
  • Deters phone theft by making stolen devices harder to resell.
  • Empowers communities to protect themselves and support law enforcement.

AI Advantage (Future Feature)

In the future, NoStolen will use AI to detect fraud, analyze suspicious patterns, and provide smart alerts for authorities. This will help stop thieves from gaming the system and make the platform even more secure.
